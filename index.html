About Us

KHALIFA ABDULLAH ALALI FOR CONTRACTING (W.L.L.) is established to provide engineering, fabrication, maintenance and project execution services across Qatar. Our main facilities are located at Mesaieed MIC Industrial Area with 40,000 square meters of infrastructure including 6 workshops, fabrication shops, maintenance units and office facilities.
